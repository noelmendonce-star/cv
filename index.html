CV Tailor

Tailor your CV to a specific job description. Reword bullets to match the job's language, get suggestions on which experience to emphasise, and see exactly what changed before you download. Runs entirely in your browser — your CV never leaves your device for parsing, and your API key stays in this tab.

How to get a Claude API key (one-time setup, ~5 min)
  1. Go to console.anthropic.com and sign up.
  2. Add a small amount of credit. About $5 covers ~150 CVs.
  3. Open the "API Keys" page and create a new key. It starts with sk-ant-.
  4. Paste it below. Your key stays in this browser tab only — it's sent directly to Anthropic, never to anyone else.
